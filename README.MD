# Version 1.0:
4 new types of slime: air, water, earth, fire. Each type has its own behavioral specifics  
### Air slime:
![](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/1188/599/2025-05-15_02-10-26-jpg.jpg)
- Can be found on mountain tops as well as above 160 blocks
- Can do double jump
- Jumps higher than other slimes
- A Wind Charge can... _Indecipherable handwriting_

### Water slime:
![](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/1188/606/2025-05-15_02-22-35-jpg.jpg)
- May be found in oceans and rivers. And with some chance in other bodies of water as well
- Can find and put out fires. However, there are nuances... _Indecipherable handwriting_
- Can breathe underwater and swim
- Oceanic slime has different color
- Different sized slimes may react differently to a click with bucket in hand
- Can attack fire slimes

### Earth slime:
![](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/1188/607/2025-05-15_02-05-30-jpg.jpg)
- They can be found on the plains and in forests
- Has more hp than other slimes, but has less speed
- Does not float in water

### Fire slime:
![](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/1188/609/2025-05-15_02-36-54-jpg.jpg)
- They are most often found in Nether, but with a small chance of being found in the lava lakes of the Overworld
- Damaged by water
- Immune to fire
- Can attack water slimes
- Different sized slimes may react differently to a click with bucket in hand

## Potions with new effects  
### Double Jump Potion
With this effect, the player can make another jump while in the air

## Additional potions  
You can brew a Potion of Fire Resistance (5 min duration) from Potion of Fire Resistance (3 min duration) and Fire Slime Block  
You can brew a Potion of Water Breathing (5 min duration) from Potion of Water Breathing (3 min duration) and Water Slime Block

## Glowing Slime
You can use Glow Ink Sac on new slime blocks to make the blocks glow:  
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/ntoRqlp.png" width="500">
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/ndVPXc3.png" width="500">  

(Click RMB with axe in main hand to scrape the ink off the block)

## Earth Power
You can improve your shield with earth slime ball on anvil  
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/v7tJHVN.png" width="500">

This power is able to reflect damage when you block attacks with your shield  
Compatible with shields from other mods
